Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Neura AI collects, how that information is used, and your options regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. User Input:

When you use our Neura AI, you provide text prompts, preferences (colors, layout, features), and other data to guide the AI in generating content. This input is processed by the underlying AI model (Google Gemini) to fulfill your request. We do not store your specific prompts or generated outputs on our servers after the session concludes.

1.2. API Key:

The application uses an API key (`process.env.API_KEY`) to interact with the Google Gemini API. This key is expected to be provided externally and is not collected or stored by the application itself.

1.3. Usage Data:

We may collect anonymous usage data to understand how the application is used (e.g., component interactions, page views). This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

2. How We Use Your Information
